[center][U][B][size=10][COLOR=lime]Queue Bot Information:[/COLOR][/B][/U][/center][list=1]
[*]After queueing up for the server, you may go anywhere in the Teamspeak. Chill with friends or hang with Staff!
[*]You will get poked prior to being moved.
[*]You will be removed from queue if you get moved to AFK or you disconnect from the server. You will [u]not[/u] get your queue spot back if you rejoin after 5 minutes.

[center][U][B][size=10][COLOR=green]Queue Bot Commands:[/COLOR][/B][/U][/center][list=1]
[*][b]!joinqueue <server #>[/b] - Joins the queue! [i](Example: !joinqueue X)[/i]
[*][b]!leavequeue[/b] - Leaves the queue!
[*][b]!queuelist <server #>[/b] - Lists everyone in the server queue! [i](Example: !queuelist X)[/i]
[*][b]!queuepos[/b] - Gives the number you are in queue!
[*][b]!pm[/b] - Opens a private message to the bot for commands!
[*][b][color=lime]*NEW*[/color] !recover[/b] - Recover your queue position as long as you left/disconnected from queue under 5 minutes ago. (DO NOT rejoin queue before using this command. Use it as soon as you join the queue channel!)[/list]

[center][U][B][size=10][COLOR=orange]Queue Bot Staff Commands:[/COLOR][/B][/U][/center][list=1]
[*][b]!setqueue[/b] <uniqueID> <#> - Set someone's queue position. Person needs to be in a queue before running this command. [i](Example: !setqueue PJYZN4UcWKNgw4JpCNMe08Frvec= 1)[/i]
[*][b]!queuekick[/b] <uniqueID> - Kick someone from the queue! [i](Example: !queuekick PJYZN4UcWKNgw4JpCNMe08Frvec=)[/i]
[*][b]!forcequeue[/b] <#> - Manually process the queue by ignoring people in 10-1. [i](Example: !forcequeue 1)[/i]
[*][b][color=red]*ADMIN*[/color] !nukequeue[/b] <#> - Nuke a server's queue. [i](Example: !nukequeue X)[/i]
[*][b][color=red]*ADMIN*[/color] !messagequeue[/b] <#> <message> - Message everyone in a server's queue. [i](Example: !messagequeue X Queue Bot restarting!)[/i]
[*][b][color=red]*ADMIN*[/color] !reload[/b] <command> - Reload a command! [i](Example: !reload setqueue)[/i][/list]
